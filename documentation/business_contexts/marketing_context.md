# Marketing Dataset — Business Context

## Scenario
You are a data analyst at a mid-sized e-commerce retailer. The marketing department has provided a dataset of **600,000 customer records** covering demographics, digital engagement, purchase behavior, and campaign response patterns collected over a three-year period.

The company is experiencing increasing customer churn and needs help understanding:
- Which customers are likely to leave, and what drives churn
- How to estimate the long-term revenue potential of each customer
- How to segment the customer base for targeted campaigns
- Where to invest marketing resources for the best return

Your team has been asked to analyze this data and present findings to the Chief Marketing Officer.

## Key Business Questions
- **Retention**: Can we identify at-risk customers before they churn?
- **Valuation**: How much is each customer worth over their lifetime?
- **Segmentation**: Are there distinct customer groups that respond differently to marketing?
- **Optimization**: Which channels and campaigns are most effective?

## Your Deliverables
Use the data dictionary and analytical questions provided to guide your analysis. Present your findings as if briefing a non-technical executive audience.
